名称

svnadmin lstxns — 打印所有未提交的事物名称。

概要

svnadmin lstxns REPOS_PATH

描述

Print the names of all uncommitted transactions. See 第 5.4.3.2 节 “删除终止的事务” for information on how uncommitted transactions are created and what you should do with them.

例子

List all outstanding transactions in a repository:

$ svnadmin lstxns /var/svn/repos/ 
1w
1x
